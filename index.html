👕👗 Style Blog Review 👗👕

CASUAL WEDDING ATTIRE FOR WOMEN

We’ve arrived at the most relaxed of all wedding attires. You may think that anything goes for this dress code, but we’re here to tell you that’s not the case. With the rise of backyard ceremonies, casual wedding attire for women is becoming increasingly more common. A wedding is still a special occasion that requires more effort than your everyday informal ensemble. Typically, a wedding with a casual dress code takes place during the day or outdoors. Give spring wedding guest dresses or skirt sets a whirl paired with wedges and delicate jewelry.This wedding dress code comes with more flexibility than others. As long as you abide by the preferred dress code, you’ll look and feel your best while celebrating the newlyweds. Cheers!

Picnic in the Park

Pack your favorite snacks into a picnic basket, grab a blanket and head to the park for fresh air and vitamin D. Complement a pair of cropped wide-leg linen pants with an airy cotton blouse adorned with handcrafted embroidered detailing. Perfect your simplistic yet impactful ensemble with leather sandals. Then, kick off your sandals and bury your feet in the grass. You’ll feel rejuvenated and grounded after a lunch date with Mother Nature.

Groovy Style: The 1960s

Looking to head even farther back in time for more fashion inspiration? We’ve got you. Many of the 2022 fashion trends for women are borrowing tips straight from the mod style of the 60s. This era arguably redefined the fashion world and is responsible for a heavy influence of bold print and bright color. And, of course, we can’t forget to credit the 60s for gracing us with shorter hemlines and the iconic mini skirt. So next time you get dressed, reach for that denim cut-off mini skirt. You’ll bring the best of both the 1960s and 2000s straight into 2022.

Winter Date Night

When it comes to winter fashion 2022, sometimes less is more. The cut-out craze will still be going strong this winter, and we are here for it. For your next date night, grab a black sweater with unexpected details. Our current crush is asymmetrical shoulder cut-outs. They show the perfect peek of skin without worrying about getting frost-bite. Tuck this edgy sweater into a pale pink pleated midi skirt, and complete the look with sleek black booties. You’re all set for walking in a winter wonderland. Rock a bold lip, and don’t shy away from lingering under the mistletoe.

Staying In

Time to hit the couch to binge-watch some rom coms (date’s choice, of course). This look strikes a fine balance of being comfortable without looking like it’s laundry day. Enter the altheisure movement. A t-shirt paired with some type of jogger pant or chino is a relaxed look, but without the frump. Slip on your favorite kicks and add a layer that’s minimal but not boring as the finale.

Going Out

You’re probably thinking that this is a no brainer—just throw on the usual suspects: a t-shirt and jeans. However, this is actually the time to step up your style game. We’re not suggesting a suit (you don’t want to be overdressed), but instead, take your closet’s heavy hitters out for a spin. Try this go-to outfit equation: blazer, sweater, dress shirt, chinos, oxfords. You already appreciate how these pieces look on you, and now, your date will too.

Weekend Brunch

It’s daytime, so your outfit will be seen—every part of it—but you got this. Start by taking your normal Saturday look up a notch. A plaid shirt goes a long way; a flannel option goes even further if it’s cold and outdoor seating is the only option. Experiment with colorful chinos, but keep the color in the soft zone—nothing you’re wearing should be too loud. You could go the sneakers route, but why not raise the bar with a pair of oxfords? You just may be the best-dressed person in the place.

Street Style

If you like testing the waters with the greatest hits of the season mixed in with your trusty closet staples, a reservation at the latest hotspot is the time and place to show off your merging skills. By building your look around a luxe jacket such as a bold quilted bomber, you won’t need a lot of other flair. A few solid layers (that fit impeccably) up top paired with colored pants and sleek boots let your outerwear hero piece shine while reinforcing your sartorial skill set—your date will definitely notice.
